Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is defined as the process of bringing about new ideas, methods, that have a significant positive impact and value

Sustainability

Sustainability is the ability to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations.

Infrastructure

Dnfrastructure is the basic facilities and system serving a country, region, or community.infrastructure include mass transit and tele-networks.

Empowerment

Empowerment means that people have power and control over their own lives, and are respected and confident in their communities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.
